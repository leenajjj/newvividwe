Vividshe Artist Levels

Empowering women artists to shine at every stage of their journey.

Level 1: Beginner

Beginner

You’re just starting your journey as an artist. Let your curiosity lead the way!

Focus: Learning basics, experimenting, and building confidence.

Level 2: Intermediate

Intermediate

You’ve gained some skills and are ready to showcase your growing talent.

Focus: Refining techniques, exploring styles, and gaining feedback.

Level 3: Advanced

Advanced

Your skills are polished, and your work inspires others. You’re ready for bigger challenges.

Focus: Mastery, creating a signature style, and mentoring others.

Level 4: Expert

Expert

You’re at the top of your game, leading in your field and shaping the future of art.

Focus: Innovating, teaching, and leaving a legacy.
